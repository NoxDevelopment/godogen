# AURETHIA — World & Lore Bible (Nox Loom MUD)

> Original GemStone-IV-*structured* setting for the `text-rpg-mud` flagship.
> This is our own world — **not** GemStone's Elanthia. It is authored to the same
> depth pattern (a woven pantheon, a real-time god-named calendar, playable races,
> a deep-time timeline, hand-authored towns ringed by tiered hunting grounds) so
> the same systems (respawn, calendar, weather, ecology, economy, societies) map
> onto it. All keys here are the canon other modules reference.

---

## The World

- **World:** **Aurethia** — a world the first gods *wove* into being upon the
  **Great Loom** at the heart of creation. Reality is a **Weave** of countless
  threads; each thread is a strand of fate braided with raw **mana**. To work
  magic is to pluck the Weave; to die is to have one's thread cut — and, by the
  grace of Mordwyn, sometimes re-knotted.
- **Continent:** **Ossaneth** — the known continent, a rugged northern frontier
  of failing kingdoms, old ruins and untamed wilds on the shore of **Harrowmere
  Bay**. Beyond the **Dragontooth Range** to the east lie the elven cities; to
  the south, the remnants of the human **Vandric Empire**; sea-lanes west run to
  isles not yet charted in play.
- **The Cutters' threat:** the dark gods (the *Sundered*) are **Threadcutters**
  who would unravel the Weave entirely. Their servant-cults seed undeath and
  madness at the edges of the map — the living-world antagonist layer.

## Cosmology in one paragraph

The **Weavers** (the first gods) spun Aurethia on the Great Loom and set the
lesser gods — the **Arkoni** — to tend it. In the deep past the **Devourers**
(the *Hollow Ones*) tore a rent in the sky and warred to *eat* the Weave's mana;
the war nearly unmade the world and did unmake most of the elder Weavers,
freeing the Arkoni to become the gods mortals now worship. The Arkoni are split
by how they would treat the Weave: preserve it (**the Radiant Weave**), rule and
exploit it (**the Sundered**), or stand apart from it (**the Grey Threads**).

## The Pantheon (the Arkoni)

### The Radiant Weave — light-aligned (preservers)
| God | Domain | Symbol |
|---|---|---|
| **Aureon** | law, kingship, order, the crown — *King of the Gods* | golden loom-cross |
| **Sylwenna** | wisdom, knowledge, the weaving of fate (Aureon's consort) | silver spindle |
| **Mordwyn** | death, rebirth, winter, safe passage — *keeper of the Ivory Gate* | ivory key |
| **Thessaly** | nature, harvest, healing, autumn | sheaf of grain |
| **Baen Torvald** | the forge, craft, stone (patron of dwarves) | veiled hammer |
| **Solheim** | the sun, summer, fire | blazing disc |
| **Eluvaine** | love, spring, fertility (patron of halflings) | white rose |
| **Vaeric** | roads, travel, messengers, luck | silver hart |
| **Nyssa** | night, dreams, sleep — chief foe of the dark gods | crescent veil |
| **Kaethis** | strength, valor, honest combat | iron gauntlet |
| **Maroth** | the seas, storms, tides | coral trident |
| **Cael** / **Iriel** | festivals & music / art & prophecy (twins) | laughing mask / open eye |

**Mordwyn** is the resurrection deity — her **Ivory Gate** (the arch in her
temple) is the respawn/deliverance anchor. Harrowgate is her town.

### The Sundered — dark-aligned (Threadcutters)
| God | Domain | Symbol |
|---|---|---|
| **Malgra** | tyranny, domination, darkness — *Queen of the Sundered* | black crown |
| **Vorlyx** | undeath, lies, soul-binding — raiser of the dead | green coiled serpent |
| **Xeru** | nightmares, madness, terror | black jackal |
| **Thann'Vok** *the Unmaker* | annihilation, demon-summoning — would *cut the Loom* | six-rayed void-star |
| **Ghorros** | suffering, torture | barbed chain |
| **Karneth** | bloodlust, war-frenzy | red scimitar |
| **Ossivane** *the Grandfather* | forbidden knowledge, fire-theft | burning book |

**The Sundered Triad** — **Vorlyx + Xeru + Thann'Vok** — is the destruction axis
behind cataclysms and undead incursions. An uneasy truce brokered by Aureon
keeps open war between Radiant and Sundered from erupting. These gods drive the
faction-pressure / incursion events: **Vorlyx → undead**, **Xeru → madness
events**, **Thann'Vok → demon raids**.

### The Grey Threads — neutral
| God | Domain | Symbol |
|---|---|---|

| **Zaleth** | the moons, chaos, "unlucid freedom" | fractured moon |

## The Elanthian-style calendar (1:1 real-time)

Time passes at real-world rate; months and days are named for the gods. In-world
year = real year + 1000 (the Loom-Reckoning); so 2026 CE ≈ **3026 L.R.**

- **Months (Jan→Dec):** Mordwyn, Ossivane, Maroth, Eluvaine, Karneth, Sylwenna,
  Aureon, Solheim, Thessaly, Iriel, Baen Torvald, Malgra.
- **Days (Sun→Sat):** Restday, Volnesday, Kaethis' Day, Sylwennight, Marothday,
  Day of the Hart, Feastday.
- The month's patron god themes that month's holidays; the **wandering merchant**
  circuits to Ravenholt every **Feastday**.

## Playable races (roster)

Human, Half-Elf, High Elf, Dusk Elf (exiled dark-elf house), Dwarf, Halfling,
Hollow Gnome, Burgh Gnome, Half-Ogran (giant-blooded), Sylvan Elf, and the
**Erevani** — a tall, thin, faintly-elven people who *appeared* on Ossaneth's
shores only ~1,500 years ago from no known land (a ready-made "outsider" hook).
Each race carries stat modifiers + naming/appearance conventions, bound to a
Companion-AI cultural origin so generated citizens get authentic names/behavior.

## Timeline / eras (deep-lore backbone)

1. **Age of the Loom** — the Weavers spin Aurethia; the Arkoni serve them.
2. **The Devourer War** (~100,000 yrs ago) — the Hollow Ones rend the sky to eat
   the Weave; the Weavers win but are nearly destroyed, freeing the Arkoni.
3. **The Long Dark** — an Arkoni-ruled interregnum; monsters roam freely.
4. **The Elven Ascendancy** — the first mortal empire; the Seven Houses; ends in
   the House Wars and the exile of the **Dusk Elves** (a house that summoned a
   demon and was cast out — our dark-elf origin).
5. **The Vandric Empire** — a human empire spreads from the south; wars, then
   slow decline, leaving the northern frontier (Ossaneth) to fend for itself.
6. **The Loom-Reckoning (Modern Era)** — the present. Signature living saga:
   **the Harrowmere Blight** — a Vorlyxian cult raising the drowned dead beneath
   Harrowgate (the template for a recurring, town-threatening auto-saga; seeded
   already in the Ratwarren's deeps).

## Geography built in play (see `world/nox_world.py`)

- **Harrowgate** — starter human frontier **harbor town** on Harrowmere Bay;
  patron **Mordwyn**; central square hub + full service set + Moot Hall + docks.
- **Mistwood Forest / The King's Road** — the misty wilderness travel stretch
  north (with a **wayshrine of Vaeric**), linking the two towns.
- **Ravenholt** — walled inland **trade town** on the plain; patron **Aureon**;
  built around the **Grand Bazaar** and a **Caravan Yard**.
- **The Ratwarren** — the starter **dungeon**: flooded sewer-catacombs beneath
  Harrowgate's south gate, tiered 0→3, where Vorlyx's blight seeps up from below.

Travel: *Harrowgate square → North Gate → King's Road → Mistwood → Ravenholt.*
