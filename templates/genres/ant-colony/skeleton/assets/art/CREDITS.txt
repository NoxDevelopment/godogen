NOTES
-----
- The ants are generic top-down "bugs" (rounded body, 2 eyes, 4 splayed legs),
  not literally ant-shaped, but read clearly as small insects from above and
  come in 4 colors x 2 sizes -- ideal for worker/queen/rival differentiation.
- No genuinely CC0 *ant-specific* top-down sprite was found; the dedicated
  ant sprites on OpenGameArt ("16x16 Ant Enemies", "Walking Ant") are CC0 but
  SIDE-VIEW, so they were not used for this top-down game.
- _ASSET_OVERVIEW.png is a preview montage (not a game asset).
